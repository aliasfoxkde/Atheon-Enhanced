## Community Impact

Every contribution benefits both projects:
- **Pattern contributions** apply to both official and enhanced versions
- **Bug fixes** improve stability for all users
- **Documentation** helps everyone understand the tools
- **Testing** ensures quality across all versions

---

## How to Get Here

### For Official Project
1. Open a pull request in [HoraDomu/Atheon](https://github.com/HoraDomu/Atheon)
2. Merge a pattern, or fix a bug
3. Your name goes here permanently

### For Enhanced Fork
1. Open a pull request in [aliasfoxkde/Atheon](https://github.com/aliasfoxkde/Atheon)
2. Add experimental features, patterns, or improvements
3. Your contribution appears in the [contributors graph](https://github.com/aliasfoxkde/Atheon/graphs/contributors)

---

## Recognition

- **All contributors** are recognized in both projects
- **Pattern authors** are credited in pattern files
- **Significant contributions** are highlighted in documentation
- **Community members** are valued and appreciated

Thank you to everyone who contributes to making Atheon better for all users!
